Jam'iyah Global (Canada) is a Canadian-registered non-profit social service organisation dedicated to strengthening social, educational, spiritual, and humanitarian support for Muslim communities across North America.

Jam'iyah Global (Canada) aims to provide support to the wider Muslim community in North America, particularly Canada, through programmes in religious and secular education support, family development, mental health, youth empowerment, and social upliftment.
